Welcome to SG prayer. This is a simple app that lets your small group keep track of prayer requests in a given week. To register, simply add your group's name and the day you meet. Once you register, your link will be www.church.longlined.com/[your-name]. Have members add requests during your prayer time or throughout the week, and check back at your link throughout the week to pray for your small group.

If you split into prayer groups during small group, the requests list for this week will udpdate automatically as people add requests, so there's no need to ever refresh.
